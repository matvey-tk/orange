We are a team at AUT looking at businesses around the world and seeing who is stuck in yesterday's technology.

We believe there are gaps in incorporation all around.
Technology is growing but many resist the change.

We would like to investigate why this is and what fields in particular are lacking.

We believe there are ethical and legal cases but the majority may just be ignorant and scared to try move forward.
